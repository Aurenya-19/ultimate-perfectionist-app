1. Physical Quantities

Physical quantities are quantities that can be measured. They are of two types:

  • Fundamental Quantities: Length, Mass, Time, Temperature, Current, Luminous Intensity, Amount of Substance
  • Derived Quantities: Area, Volume, Velocity, Acceleration, Force, etc.

2. SI Units

Length: meter (m)
Mass: kilogram (kg)
Time: second (s)
Temperature: kelvin (K)
Current: ampere (A)
Luminous Intensity: candela (cd)
Amount of Substance: mole (mol)
