Deep Learning System

6 Proven Learning Techniques

üéì
Feynman Technique

Explain concepts in simple terms as if teaching someone else. This reveals knowledge gaps and ensures true understanding.

  • Choose a concept and write it down
  • Explain it in simple language
  • Identify gaps in your understanding
  • Review and simplify further
üìÖ
Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals for long-term retention.

  • Review after 1 day
  • Review after 3 days
  • Review after 1 week
  • Review after 2 weeks
  • Review after 1 month
üß†
Active Recall

Test yourself without looking at notes. Actively retrieve information from memory.

  • Close your book/notes
  • Write down everything you remember
  • Check and fill gaps
  • Practice with problems
üó∫Ô∏è
Mind Mapping

Visualize connections between concepts to see the big picture.

  • Start with main concept in center
  • Branch out to related topics
  • Connect related ideas
  • Use colors and symbols
üîÄ
Interleaving

Mix different topics instead of blocking. Strengthens learning and connections.

  • Don't study one topic for hours
  • Switch between related topics
  • Mix problem types
  • Compare and contrast
üí≠
Elaboration

Connect new knowledge to existing knowledge. Ask "why" and "how".

  • Ask "Why does this work?"
  • Ask "How does this relate to X?"
  • Find real-world applications
  • Create analogies
