Feynman Technique

Explain concepts in simple terms as if teaching someone else.

  • Choose a concept and write it down
  • Explain it in simple language
  • Identify gaps in your understanding
  • Review and simplify further

Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals for long-term retention.

  • Review after 1 day
  • Review after 3 days
  • Review after 1 week
  • Review after 2 weeks
  • Review after 1 month

Active Recall

Test yourself without looking at notes.

  • Close your book/notes
  • Write down everything you remember
  • Check and fill gaps
  • Practice with problems

  • Branch out to related topics

  • Use colors and symbols

  • Switch between related topics

  • Compare and contrast
